What is the NRHS Yearly Athletic Pass?

By purchasing an athletic pass, you gain admission to all athletic games and competitions for the entire year. There are four tiers of passes that you can purchase at the front desk of the bookstore. Keep clicking to learn more about each pass.

Individual Adult Pass

Adult passes are $75 and include one adult admission to any home high school event. Pass valid for general admission seating only. Preferred seating passes are an additional $30. Click here to see stadium and gymnasium seating.

Individual Student Pass

Student passes are $35 and include one student admission to any home high school event. Pass valid for general admission seating only. Passes for OHSAA and Conference tournaments are an additional $30. Click here for tournament schedules.

Family Pass

A family pass is $200 and includes admission for 2 adults and 2 students to any home high school event. Pass valid for general admission seating only. You may purchase additional student passes in order to accomodate for your whole family.

Senior Pass

Residents of North Ridgeville who are 65+ years old may obtain a free Senior Pass from North Ridgeville High School. The card entitles the card holder to free admission to all home North Ridgeville athletic contests. Proof of residency required.
